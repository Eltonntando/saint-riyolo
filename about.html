About Me

Born as Mbongeni Ngomane in the vibrant province of Mpumalanga, I go by the name Saint-Riyolo — I'm a dynamic Dj known for my electrifying House | Amapiano | Jazz | Soul & RnB music, authentic kasi lifestyle sounds, and powerful entertainment. With a unique blend of musical prowess and captivating performances, bringing a fresh and compelling sound to the entertainment scene. Hailing from Nkomazi representing Mzinti 1352, all the way to Mbombela and the Mpumalanga province as a whole.
Hailing from Nkomazi and proudly representing Mzinti 1352 and beyond, I bring the rich cultural tapestry of Mpumalanga to the music and entertainment scene. My cool, calm, and collected demeanor reflects my deep love for people, groove culture, nature, God, and the arts. These inspirations fuel my creativity, driving me to craft sets that resonates on a profound level.
I’m on a mission to become one of the most influential Dj and businessman to emerge from MP, and I’m just getting started. Trust me—this journey is going to be unforgettable.
